Lamborghini Sián FKP 37

The Sián FKP 37 is Lamborghini’s first hybrid super sports car, blending a powerful V12 engine with a supercapacitor for electrifying performance. Limited to 63 coupes and 19 roadsters, it’s a masterpiece of design and technology.

Iconic Features

The Sián FKP 37 redefines automotive excellence with cutting-edge technology and bold design.

  • Carbon-Fiber Monocoque: Ultra-lightweight chassis for superior rigidity and agility, crafted with aerospace-grade materials.
  • Y-Shaped Headlights: Iconic LED design inspired by the Terzo Millennio concept, enhancing aerodynamics and visibility.
  • Smart Material Cooling Vanes: Active rear vanes open automatically using temperature-sensitive materials, optimizing airflow.
  • Supercapacitor Hybrid System: Stores ten times the power of a lithium-ion battery, delivering instant electric boost.
  • Aerodinamica Lamborghini Attiva (ALA): Advanced aerodynamics system adjusts downforce and drag for optimal performance.
  • Regenerative Braking: Converts kinetic energy into electric power, enhancing efficiency and performance.
  • Exclusive Paint Finishes: Unique Ad Personam colors with metallic and pearlescent effects for a striking appearance.

Luxurious Interior

The Sián FKP 37’s interior is a blend of futuristic design and Italian craftsmanship, offering an immersive driving experience.

  • Premium Materials: Alcantara and leather upholstery with carbon-fiber accents, hand-stitched for ultimate luxury.
  • 3D-Printed Air Vents: Customizable vents created with additive manufacturing, a first in automotive design.
  • Digital Cockpit: Fully customizable digital dashboard with high-resolution displays for driver information.
  • Ad Personam Customization: Tailor every detail, from stitching colors to exclusive material combinations.
  • Ergonomic Design: Driver-focused cabin with sculpted seats for comfort during high-performance driving.
